Operational Notes & Assumptions

Methodology: This calculator determines the total salts needed to hit your final mineral profile. It then splits these salts proportionally between the mash and sparge water based on volumes, and estimates the resulting mash pH.

Post-Boil Acid: To counter the pH rise from heavy dry hopping, a lower knockout pH is recommended. The calculator estimates the grams of 88% Lactic Acid to add at the end of the boil to reach this modern target.

Target Profile: Calibrated for a soft, hop-forward beer style, aiming for a final water profile of 100 ppm Sulfate (SO₄) and 150 ppm Chloride (Cl).
